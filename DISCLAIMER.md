# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides Swedish veterinary medicine data for informational purposes only.

## Not Veterinary Medical Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute veterinary medical advice. Withdrawal periods, prescribing cascade rules, and banned substance lists are based on published Swedish and EU regulatory sources, but individual treatment decisions require professional veterinary clinical judgement. Always verify withdrawal periods against the current SPC (Summary of Product Characteristics) from Lakemedelsverket before administering any veterinary medicine to food-producing animals. Incorrect withdrawal periods can lead to food chain contamination and regulatory enforcement.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **Lakemedelsverket** (Swedish Medical Products Agency) -- Veterinary medicines register, authorised products, SPCs
- **Jordbruksverket** (Swedish Board of Agriculture) -- Prescribing cascade rules, record-keeping requirements, animal health regulations
- **EMA** (European Medicines Agency) -- Union Product Database for centrally authorised veterinary medicines

Data is derived from Swedish public records under offentlighetsprincipen and EU open data policies.

## Currency

Veterinary medicine authorisations change. New products are authorised, existing products are withdrawn, and withdrawal periods are updated. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Run `check_data_freshness` to see the age of the data. The staleness threshold is 90 days.

## Jurisdiction

This server covers Sweden (SE) only. EU-wide centrally authorised products are included where valid in Sweden. This server does not cover other Nordic or EU member states.
